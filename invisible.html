A ghost story about a motorcycle accident, lost love, and the meaning of life and/or death. After making a sketchy deal with the devil, a girl whose life was cut tragically short emerges into the world as a ghost, and sets about trying to both make sense of her death and find a way to rest in peace.

Made for NaNoRenO 2013, although I ended up taking a bit more than a month in the end.
