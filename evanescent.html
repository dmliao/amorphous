Evanescent

A whimsical, flash-based bullet hell shooter that takes you through 2 parallel worlds in a child’s dream.


Play Online

Travel through 6 hand-drawn levels, and switch between a world of grey and a world of color to dodge attacks and defeat enemies. Challenge huge bosses to collect the mirror shards needed to let a child’s dream unfold.
